Mission

Guide the little fish through a polluted ocean. Collect floating trash to earn points and heal the ocean, but avoid toxic clouds that harm you and spread pollution.

Tips

  • Collect trash items to increase score and ocean health.
  • Avoid dark pollution clouds – they damage you and the ocean.
  • Watch marine animals; they are friendly but can block your path.
  • As the ocean recovers, water becomes clearer and coral starts to grow again.
